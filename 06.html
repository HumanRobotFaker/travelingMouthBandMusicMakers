VI.

     Around four in the morning, when half of Hector’s living room wall was covered in crayon drawings, Andrew’s AAAs pushed him to suggest we foment a democratic revolution amongst the hypointel students at the BeaverCreek EduCational Center.

     “Why in Kur would we do that?” I asked.

     “Destabilization, primarily.”

     “For lulz?”

     “To increase the number of potential skouts to serve the Antarctic Kingdom,” Andrew said, taping up a new crayon drawing. “Hard to get them working for us when they’re incarcerated [7x180=] 1,260 hours a year.”

     I went upstairs to check on Hector (alive; asleep) and came back down to Jakob Rums microwaving his iContactTM stream onto the LCDTV.

     “Who’s that?” I asked.

     “Harold Cox,” Jakob said. Jakob was 150 centimeters tall, had blueish-white skin, and a head full of tightly bound blonde curly locks. “The new head principal of the BeaverCreek EduCation Center.”

     “According to Antarctic Intelligence Agencies,” Jakob said, “Mr. Cox attained his newfound position as the result of his past success and his connection with several BeaverCreek SkoolBo’rd members that attend his non-sectarian yeshian temple off of FS-35 in east Dayton.”

     “It’s not what you know, but whom ya know; am I right?” Andrew said.

     “What we know,” Jakob continued, “is that he has a particularly narcissistic authoritarian style that involves imposing a whole bunch of new rules and regulations, while self-congratulating himself multiple times a day during what has been termed 15-minute PublicAddressSystem [sermons].”

     “So he’s sowing discontent amongst the normie population who just want to chew gum, watch BoobTubeTM, and space out on InfiniSpace while teachers label them ‘burgeoning proficient’ just to pass them along,” Andrew said.

     “Sounds like the perfect revolutionary proletariat class of warriors,” I laugh. “Do we have a leader or leaders of this lumpenproletariat?”

     Jakob blinked. “We have some candidates.”
