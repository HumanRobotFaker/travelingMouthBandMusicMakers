Asleep


"Hey, my mom's home," you say.

She smirks. "So?"

"You gotta put on clothes. The doorknobs don't have a lock."

"Wouldn't that be hot if she walked in?"

You panic. "No, no, no. It would not."

"Someone protest too much."

Suddenly the door starts jiggling. You throw your body into the door to keep it closed, but the door keeps pushing open.
