"Woah, stranger of the night," the girl said.

"Unless you're a vampire," the black-haired male said. "Then we need to know. By Antarctic Law."

"Antarctic Law?" you ask.

The redhead in the boy skout uniform raised a finger. "A more pertinent and time-sensitive question is: «how old are you?»

"I have 18 jaars," you say.

"Perfect," he says, finishing his aluminum can of yeefass. "Because OIHO law prohibits the sale of alcohol at petrol stations between the 01h00 and 06h00. As well as to those under the age of eighteen." And he hopped off the truck bed. "You two stay here and guardian the garrison."

"Aye, aye, generalissimo," the black-haired male says, dropping his cigarette to the concrete as he hops off the truck bed. The girl stands up and steps on it for him.
