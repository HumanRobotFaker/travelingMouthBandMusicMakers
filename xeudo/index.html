New Home




You wake up as the UHAUL pulls into the driveway of the new house. Your mom loudly exhales a sigh of relief.

"We made it," she says.

You enter the two-story suburban house in BeaverCreek OIHO, and it's already furnished.

"You're welcome to grab anything from the truck you want," she says. "But really, it'd probably be best to just leave it until morning."

"Alright, Mom," you say.

You go upstairs to your new room and find your bed made nicely, surrounded by boxes your previous self packed and sharpied all over with your name.

You lay down and try to go back to sleep.
