Drive Back


You didn't know OIHO state law, but you figured it was probably illegal to sit behind the driver's wheel of your moving vehicle while drinking yeefass. Even if the robot was temporarily in control. But you don't say anything.

"Help yourself to one," he says.

You do. It tastes surprisingly good. You've never had one before. You always thought it was for hypointels.

"How long have you lived here?" you ask.

"Most of my life. My parents were stationed at the AeroForce base, and retired here. They said they might leave once I graduate."

"How far away is that?"

Andrew shrugged his shoulders. "It depends when I get drafted."

"Drafted?"

Andrew threw a dismissive hand. "I don't feel like getting all into it."

The truck drove itself back to the driveway. It stopped and turned off.

"Where are your friends?" you ask.

"Probably fooling around inside."

"Fooling around?"

"Come on. Let's see if we can catch a free show."
