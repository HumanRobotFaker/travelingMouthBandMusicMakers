Am I here?

Where am I?

The world is make believe. I do realize that it appears consistent, tangible, and permanent. But it is not.

The vision I envision advises me to not view myself as a prisoner within a inescapable prison cell. Look, I just tried the lock! There is no lock! The door swings open freely. I dance outward and instead of meeting menacing guards poking [exothermic propulsion devices] at me, they dance gayly in celebration of my newfound freedom. The walls of the prison evaporate and I am in an open field, frolicking without fear of ticks, fire ants, snake, or spider.

You apply your energies to solving a puzzle that doesn't actually exist. That cannot be solved. Simply walk away from the table and all liberation is yours.           ###

In the XYZ room, strangers appear and disappear like gaseous bubbles at the bottom of a container of water, set atop a flame. I forget about the strangers and think about the bubbles:- suddenly enfused with energy, they bounce with great force against their neighbors, jettisoning them at great speeds, which cause them to bounce against other molecules with great force, and suddenly there's elbow room. Suddenly there's a greater vacuum of space betwixit these water molecules. And the particles found within in this newfound vacuum of space find themselves -- within this small sphere of liberation -- floating upward and upward and upward, until they reach the top of the surface. The bubble pops and the particle flies upward, angled but in a straight line. Flying at a velocity unimaginable within the electromagnetic stranglehold of close quarters with molecules that have dipole moments.

Humans cannot predict the weather; there are simply too many variables involved, too many variables to measure and for which to account. So we surrender the calculations and predictions to electricity, with these nigh-infinite loops we symbolize as [ Z E R O ] or [ O N E ], depending upon whether or not the switch is open or closed. At the speed of light, this mysterious oscillation of electric and magnetic disturbances whose velocity through empty space none can surpass, the open and closed loop reflections, send signals and mysteriously output predictions like magic. Like a god whose interior we cannot access nor fathom.

It feels as if my imagination is imprisoned by the limitation of the senses and the physical body. I cannot "see" the same frequencies as the bird due to the differences in our optical cones. I cannot imagine having physical senses that I do not have. Or is this simply foolishness? Is it an imaginary prison that I could dissipate with a simple impulse of the mind?           ###
