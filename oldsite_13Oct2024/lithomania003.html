     After cleaning up the debris above the bedrock, Andrew and Hector set to the task of breaking the impasse. Andrew used a powerdrill loaded with diamond bits to create a line of small holes in the rock. Then Hector loaded feathers-and-wedges into the holes, before the two of them took turns hammering the feathers-and-wedges deeper. Eventually, the bedrock began cracking.

     They drilled more lines of holes. Hammered more feathers-and-wedges. Eventually they were able to chip away at certain sections of bedrock and use the mattock to begin pulling apart the broken rock.
