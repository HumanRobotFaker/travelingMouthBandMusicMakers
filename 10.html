     All my life, the backyard had been our dog’s domain: dirt, weeds, patches of grass, torn up dog toys, faeces, and piss. But now that he’s dead, it’s all been steamrolled into beautification, as if he never lived at all.

     “Looks great, Mom,” I said. “I bet you’re really proud.”
